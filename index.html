SHAMAAT HOPE REVIVE INITIATIVE (SHRI)

To uplift vulnerable groups — especially women, young girls, and Almajirai — by providing them with the tools, skills, and support they need to live healthy, independent, and productive lives in their communities.

Aims and Objectives

Our mission-driven focus to economically empower women through skills, resources, and advocacy.

Economic Empowerment

Train women in business skills like marketing, budgeting, and leadership to build financial independence.

Support Women-led Businesses

Provide start-up capital, tools, and resources to help women launch and grow sustainable businesses.

Mentorship & Networking

Connect women with mentors and networks to support business growth and leadership development.

Enhance Digital Literacy

Boost digital skills to improve visibility, global participation, and technological competitiveness.

Advocate for Rights

Champion policies and laws that protect and promote the economic rights of women in all sectors.

Tailored Interventions

Develop focused strategies to empower vulnerable and marginalized women economically.

Support and Educate Almajirai

Transforming lives through nutrition, dignity, education, and mental well-being for Almajirai children.

Nutritious Meals

Ensure access to balanced daily meals to combat hunger and improve children’s health.

Clothing & Footwear

Provide essential clothes and shoes to protect, uplift dignity, and keep children safe.

Life Skills Training

Teach hygiene, time management, money handling, and communication for real-world readiness.

Health & Wellness

Provide health checkups and mental health support to nurture total child well-being.

Promote Hygiene & Self-Development

Helping young women thrive through hygiene, self-awareness, and mentorship.

Hygiene Education

Teach menstrual care, personal cleanliness, and hygiene practices for dignity and health.

Essential Supplies

Distribute sanitary pads, soap, toothpaste, and other key products to promote well-being.

Self-Development

Run sessions on confidence, emotional resilience, and personal goal-setting.

Mentorship

Connect girls with successful women leaders to inspire and support their journey.

Our Vision

To build a society where every woman, child, and marginalized youth is confident, skilled, and empowered to live a meaningful and independent life.

Our Mission

To improve the lives of underserved groups in Gombe State by providing them with practical support — such as food, training, tools, health services, and mentorship — that helps them rise above poverty, exclusion, and vulnerability.
